Smart thermostats vs. zoned smart control vs. full BMS/VRF integration

Two ways to get there: swap in smart thermostats and sensors with no rewiring needed, or integrate a BMS gateway with your VRF/ducted system during first fix for whole-building zoning.

  Smart Thermostats Zoned Smart Control Full BMS/VRF Integration
What it is Wi-Fi smart thermostats replace existing AC controllers, app scheduling Smart thermostats + occupancy/door sensors per zone, linked via a hub for scene-based control BMS gateway integrated with your VRF/ducted system — zoning dampers individually addressable
Wiring None — fits existing AC wiring Sensor wiring per zone — often wireless sensors Damper actuator + BMS bus wiring to every zone — first-fix only
Control App + schedules App, schedules, occupancy-based automatic setback App, schedules, whole-home scenes — synced with curtains & lighting
Scenes & automation Per-zone schedules only Cross-device scenes (e.g. curtains close + AC adjusts before peak heat) Whole-building zoning, fastest response, energy reporting
Voice control Often (brand-dependent) Yes Yes
Best for Apartments, single/few AC units Most villas — multiple AC zones Large villas/commercial with VRF or ducted central AC
Typical price per zone AED 800-1,800 AED 1,800-3,500 (+ hub ~1,000-2,500) AED 3,500-7,000 (+ BMS gateway & programming ~10,000-25,000)

These are general market indicative figures, not Harman's confirmed pricing — your design pack confirms the right tier, exact zone count and pricing for your home.
