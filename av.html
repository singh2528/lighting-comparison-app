Wireless speakers vs. multi-room audio vs. full AV distribution

Two ways to get there: add wireless speakers with no rewiring needed, or wire in-ceiling speakers and a central rack during first fix for whole-home distribution and a cinema room.

  Wireless Speakers Multi-Room Wired Audio Full AV Distribution
What it is Wi-Fi/Bluetooth smart speakers per room, controlled via app In-ceiling/in-wall speakers wired to a multi-zone amplifier, app + voice control Distributed AV matrix feeding speakers, TVs and a dedicated cinema room from a central rack
Wiring None — just power Speaker cable to each zone — first-fix or accessible ceiling Speaker + HDMI/AV cabling to every zone and the AV rack — first-fix only
Control App + voice (per speaker) App, voice, keypad — synced playback across zones App, voice, touch panels — whole-home audio/video routing
Scenes & automation Limited — each speaker independent Cross-device scenes (e.g. "Movie night" dims lights + starts audio) Whole-home scenes incl. cinema room, multi-source routing
Voice control Yes (brand-dependent) Yes Yes
Best for Apartments, rentals, single rooms Most villas — living, dining, bedrooms, outdoor Large villas, dedicated cinema rooms, premium integration
Typical price per zone AED 800-2,000 AED 2,500-5,000 (+ amp/hub ~3,000-8,000) AED 5,000-12,000 (+ AV rack & processor ~15,000-40,000)

These are general market indicative figures, not Harman's confirmed pricing — your design pack confirms the right tier, exact zone count and pricing for your home.
