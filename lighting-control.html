Smart switches vs. scenes vs. full bus system

Two ways to get there: add smart devices on top of existing wiring with no rewiring needed, or wire everything to a dedicated control bus during first fix for the tightest integration.

  Smart Switches Smart Switches + Scenes Full Bus System (DALI/KNX)
What it is Wi-Fi smart switches/dimmers swap in for existing switches Zigbee/Wi-Fi switches + hub for scenes, schedules & voice Dedicated DALI/KNX bus wired to a central processor — every point individually addressable
Wiring None — fits existing wiring None — fits existing wiring Dedicated bus cable to every point — first-fix only
Control App + physical switch App, voice, schedules, scenes App, voice, wall keypads, schedules — fully programmable
Scenes & automation Limited, per-switch only Cross-device scenes (lights + curtains + AC) Whole-building scenes, fastest response
Voice control Often (brand-dependent) Yes Yes
Best for Rentals, finished homes, single rooms Most retrofits & apartments Villas, new builds, premium integration
Typical price per point AED 600-1,200 AED 1,200-2,200 (+ hub ~800-1,500) AED 2,500-5,000 (+ processor & programming ~12,000-30,000)

These are general market indicative figures, not Harman's confirmed pricing — your design pack confirms the right tier, exact device count and pricing for your home.
