Plug-and-play motors vs. wired motors + scenes vs. bus-integrated control

Two ways to get there: retrofit rechargeable motors onto existing tracks with no rewiring needed, or wire motors to a hub or central processor during first fix for scene-based, whole-home control.

  Plug-and-Play Motors Wired Motors + Scenes Bus-Integrated Control
What it is Rechargeable/battery motors retrofit onto existing tracks, app control Wired motors on new or replacement tracks, linked via a hub for app, voice and schedules Motors wired to a central processor — synced with lighting and AC scenes building-wide
Wiring None — rechargeable or plug-in Motor power wiring to each track — first-fix or accessible ceiling Motor + bus wiring to every track, tied to the central processor — first-fix only
Control App + remote App, voice, remote, schedules, sun/light sensor App, voice, wall keypads, whole-home scenes
Scenes & automation Per-track schedules only Cross-device scenes (e.g. curtains close + lights dim for movie night) Whole-building scenes (e.g. curtains close + AC adjusts before peak heat)
Voice control Often (brand-dependent) Yes Yes
Best for Rentals, finished homes, a few windows Most villas — most or all windows Villas/new builds wanting full lighting + AC + curtain integration
Typical price per window AED 800-1,600 AED 1,600-2,800 (+ hub/sensor ~800-2,000) AED 2,800-4,500 (+ bus module/processor ~5,000-12,000)

These are general market indicative figures, not Harman's confirmed pricing — your design pack confirms the right tier, exact window count and pricing for your home.
