The problem

Smart home shopping is broken

The architect, the electrician, the lighting shop and the automation dealer each own one piece. Nobody designs the whole system — so you end up as the unpaid project manager.

💡

Which lights?

Warm or cool? How many downlights? What's a lumen? Showrooms sell what they stock, not what your room needs.

🏷️

Which brand?

Dozens of brands across three price tiers, and nobody compares them honestly — because everyone is paid to push one.

🔌

Will it integrate?

Wired vs wireless, KNX vs Zigbee vs WiFi vs Matter. Choose wrong and your devices never talk to each other.

🔧

Who installs it?

Finding a competent integrator is pure word-of-mouth. Quality varies wildly and quotes aren't comparable.

FAQ

Common questions

Why should I pay for a design when shops give free quotes?

A shop's "free" quote is a sales document for the brands they stock — the cost is hidden inside it. Our design is brand-neutral, compares three price tiers, and gives you a BOQ any installer must quote against line-by-line. Clients typically save several times the fee in avoided overspecification alone — and the fee is credited back if you install through our network.

Do you sell or install products yourselves?

No. We design, specify and connect you to vetted installers. That separation is what keeps the recommendation honest.

What do I need to share to get started?

Ideally your architect's floor plan (PDF is fine) plus your budget range and priorities. No plan? A simple room list with sizes works for most homes.

My house is already built. Is it too late?

No — retrofit is one of our most common projects. Wireless automation and smart switching can do a lot without civil work. The design pack tells you exactly what's feasible.

Which cities do you serve?

Design and BOQ are delivered online anywhere. Installer matching depends on our network coverage in your city — ask us on WhatsApp.
