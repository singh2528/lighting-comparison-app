Wi-Fi cameras vs. PoE + NVR vs. full security & access control

Two ways to get there: add Wi-Fi cameras with no cabling needed, or run PoE cabling to every camera and entry point during first fix for local recording, smart locks and an alarm panel.

  Wi-Fi Cameras (DIY) PoE Cameras + NVR Full Security & Access Control
What it is Wi-Fi/battery cameras, app viewing, cloud storage subscription PoE cameras wired to a local NVR — app viewing, no subscription needed PoE cameras + NVR, smart door locks, door/window sensors, alarm panel and video intercom — all in one app
Wiring None — power only, Wi-Fi connection PoE cable to each camera and the NVR — first-fix or accessible ceiling PoE + access control + intercom wiring to every point — first-fix only
Control App, motion alerts App, local NVR playback, motion/zone alerts App, NVR, alarm panel, intercom, remote door unlock
Scenes & automation Limited — per-camera alerts only Cross-device scenes (e.g. motion at night triggers lights) Whole-home scenes incl. door unlock + lights + alarm arm/disarm by schedule or geofence
Voice control Often (brand-dependent) Often (brand-dependent) Yes
Best for Apartments, rentals, single entry points Most villas — perimeter + key internal areas, no recurring fees Villas/commercial wanting cameras, access control and alarm in one system
Typical price per camera AED 300-700 AED 700-1,500 (+ NVR ~1,500-3,500) AED 1,200-2,500 (+ access control & alarm panel ~8,000-20,000)

These are general market indicative figures, not Harman's confirmed pricing — your design pack confirms the right tier, exact camera count and pricing for your home.
